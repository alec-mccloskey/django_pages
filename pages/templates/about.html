A few Intersting Facts About Me

There's nothing I enjoy more than traveling and immersing myself in the cultures of people of other nations. I've come to love traveling so much, that the only items on my "Bucket List" are:

1. Visit all 50 states in the USA -- So far, I've visited 26 out of the 50 states. I'm more than half way to my goal!

2. Visit all 7 continents -- I've got 6 continents down already! Now the challenge is figuring out how in the world I'm going to get to Antarctica.

3. Live abroad for 1 year -- While visiting the city of Bourdeaux, France to explore my heritage and the birthplace of my great grandfather, I fell in love with its beauty and the simplicity of life in the surrounding villages. It was on this trip that I decided to make it a goal of mine to be able to call Bourdeaux home, someday.
